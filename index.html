My Story

Ever since I can remember, I have always been taking ideas and bringing them to fruition. No matter how many naysayers were presenting themselves, I always wanted to see my idea through.

At the age of 14, I launched my first not for profit program that brought free carnivals to children who were underprivleged, mentally and/or physically disbaled. After 4 years from launching, the program had over 100 dedicated volunteers, numerous corporate partners, and thousands of children impacted across the New York Metropolitan area.

This mindset has taken me to found and run numerous companies including building and launching The Q Truck (quesadilla food truck), Startup Village (affordable entrepreneurial housing with a 2 year waitlist), as well as multiple companies focused in the e-commerce space, Three Mountains(used clothing reselling), Masquerade(omni-channel clothing software), as well as Demere(computer vision for smart outfits).
